Our Story

Healthy Living Starts Here

Founded in 2015, Fresh Squeeze was born out of a passion for health and wellness. We believe that what you put into your body matters, which is why we use only the freshest, organic ingredients in our juices.

Our juices are cold-pressed to preserve maximum nutrients and enzymes. No added sugars, no preservatives - just pure, natural goodness in every bottle.

Visit our juice bar and experience the difference that fresh, organic ingredients can make in your health and energy levels.
